Why Mekano

Fourteen years of factory-floor learning.

Mekano is built on the foundation of Aroma Bake Equipments — a manufacturing partnership serving bakeries across India since 2012. Every machine is fabricated in-house at our Bahadurgarh plant.

We weld the metal hard so your baking stays easy. From a two-tray deck oven to a 160-tray rotary rack, everything ships with a 12-month manufacturing warranty and installation included.

Manufactured in India. Every machine fabricated in Bahadurgarh, Haryana. No re-badged imports.

12-month warranty on every unit. Repair or replacement of defective parts, direct from our service centre.

Direct-to-buyer pricing. No distributors, no markup. You talk to the factory.
